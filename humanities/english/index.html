Unnamed
Marcy 17, 2025
That pungent smell of rotten copper Shards gleaming back the sun only slightly It was a whetted blade forged By an absence of salty sweat And the presence of that rose-canary viscous liquid And the sadism of its wielders it once stood In a matter almost indefatigably Bright in the masked sun Whitish bubbles of salty sea corrosive but unchanging to its flesh They lapped at it like soft tongues And before that it was a dazzling star Its hot, naked self exposed to chill reality It is cruel tender prickly But bulbous sweet sap oozing You take a bite And say yum Forgetting the sorrow all you think about is Its saccharine taste But not sugarcoated
Girl
February 28, 2025
Up on the moonlit branch She was like a butterfly Effulgent wings broke the haze of stormy clouds But chains spread from snowy branches A skiff of snow that children hold out their tongues to It was so soft But it was so cold Stuck to that branch The ice swallowed her feet But like the old titan’s plan It falls to no avail as his children gain freedom from that accursed belly Up on the moonlit branch Yellows and greens and reds broke the horizon And she sailed free A droplet of water

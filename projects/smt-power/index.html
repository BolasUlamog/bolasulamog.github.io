SMT Power Round

Stanford Math Tournament 2026 — Problem Writing

Co-authored the Power Round for Stanford Math Tournament 2026. The power round is a proof-based team round where teams work together to explore a single mathematical topic in depth through a series of connected problems.

We actually wrote two power rounds this year—the first one ended up being too hard, so we pivoted and wrote a new one on matroids. Matroids are a beautiful abstraction that generalize the notion of linear independence from vector spaces to more general combinatorial structures.

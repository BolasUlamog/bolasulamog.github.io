Problem 8

\(\text{Find the supremum and infimum of the following sets:}\)

\(S_1=\{x\,|\,x<{2}\}\)

\(S_2=\{x\,|\,x\geq{-2}\}\)

\(S_3=\{x\,|\,x\text{ is an integer}\}\)

Reveal Answer

\(\sup{S_1}=2, \inf{S_1}=-\infty\)

\(\sup{S_2}=\infty, \inf{S_2}=-2\)

\(\sup{S_3}=\infty, \inf{S_3}=-\infty\)

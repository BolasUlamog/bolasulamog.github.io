1999 A-5

Show that the improper integral

\[\lim_{B\to\infty}\int_0^B\sin{(x)}\sin{(x^2)}\,dx\]

converges.

Click to reveal the answer

\[\text{Firstly, we know that }\sin{x}=\frac{e^{ix}-e^{-ix}}{2i}\]

\[\text{Let }I=\lim_{B\to\infty}\int_0^B\sin{(x)}\sin{(x^2)}\,dx\text{ and }\lim_{B\to\infty}\int_0^B\sin{(x)}\sin{(x^2)}\,dx=\lim_{B\to\infty}\int_0^B\frac{e^{ix}-e^{-ix}}{2i}\frac{e^{ix^2}-e^{-ix^2}}{2i}\,dx\]

\[=\lim_{B\to\infty}\int_0^B\frac{(e^{ix}-e^{-ix})(e^{ix^2}-e^{-ix^2})}{-4}\,dx=-\frac{1}{4}\lim_{B\to\infty}\int_0^B(e^{ix}-e^{-ix})(e^{ix^2}-e^{-ix^2})\,dx\]

\[=-\frac{1}{4}\lim_{B\to\infty}\int_0^Be^{ix+ix^2}-e^{ix-ix^2}-e^{-ix+ix^2}+e^{-ix-ix^2}\,dx\]

\[=-\frac{1}{4}\lim_{B\to\infty}\biggl(\int_0^Be^{i(x+x^2)}\,dx-\int_0^Be^{i(x-x^2)}\,dx-\int_0^Be^{i(-x+x^2)}\,dx+\int_0^Be^{i(-x-x^2)}\,dx\biggr)\]

\[\text{We can simplify by completeing the square for each }e^{iP(x)}:\]

\[I=-\frac{1}{4}\lim_{B\to\infty}\biggl(\int_0^Be^{i((x+\frac{1}{2})^2-\frac{1}{4})}\,dx-\int_0^Be^{-i((x-\frac{1}{2})^2+\frac{1}{4})}\,dx-\int_0^Be^{i((x-\frac{1}{2})^2-\frac{1}{4})}\,dx+\int_0^Be^{-i((x+\frac{1}{2})^2+\frac{1}{4})}\,dx\biggr)\]

\[\text{For each integral we can apply the appropriate substituion: }\]

\[I=-\frac{1}{4}\lim_{B\to\infty}\biggl(\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{i(u^2-\frac{1}{4})}\,du-\int_{-\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{-i(u^2+\frac{1}{4})}\,du-\int_{-\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{i(u^2-\frac{1}{4})}\,du+\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{-i(u^2+\frac{1}{4})}\,du\biggr)\]

\[=-\frac{1}{4}\lim_{B\to\infty}\biggl(\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{-i/4}e^{ix^2}\,dx-\int_{-\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{-i/4}e^{-ix^2}\,dx-\int_{-\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{-i/4}e^{ix^2}\,dx+\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{-i/4}e^{-ix^2}\,dx\biggr)\]

\[=-\frac{1}{4}e^{-i/4}\lim_{B\to\infty}\biggl(\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{ix^2}\,dx-\int_{-\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{ix^2}\,dx-\int_{-\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{-ix^2}\,dx+\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{-ix^2}\,dx\biggr)\]

\[=-\frac{1}{4}e^{-i/4}\lim_{B\to\infty}\biggl(\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{ix^2}\,dx-\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{ix^2}\,dx-\int_{-\frac{1}{2}}^{\frac{1}{2}}e^{ix^2}\,dx-\int_{-\frac{1}{2}}^{\frac{1}{2}}e^{-ix^2}\,dx-\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{-ix^2}\,dx+\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^Be^{-ix^2}\,dx\biggr)\]

\[=\frac{1}{4}e^{-i/4}\lim_{B\to\infty}\biggl(\int_{-\frac{1}{2}}^{\frac{1}{2}}e^{ix^2}\,dx+\int_{-\frac{1}{2}}^{\frac{1}{2}}e^{-ix^2}\,dx\biggr)\]

\[\text{This obviously converges. }\]

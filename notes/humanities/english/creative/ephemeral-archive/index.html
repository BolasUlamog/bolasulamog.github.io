Ephemeral Archive

My life is full of dreams. They come and go and most are forgotten. Of course I don’t remember the place of them either. But often they are inspired by literature and the rabbiting on of individuals and the system that we humans love to further press further on the back of the oppressed and to hear those screams isn’t commonly relevant in my dreams but oh it’s there. But here is the closest I can come to a list.

1. A long time ago

I wake up with febrile breaths and a fear of death. This happens enough such that I remember it clearer. My eyes are heavy but closing them is painful. I will wake up and be dead and forever gone, feeling nothing and being nothing. My heart will stop. I run away from my own bed to be with my mom, who was asleep.

2. Less long ago but after reading a childhood book

The book is crisp and in new condition. This part is not a dream. I see him jump in front of the train and later I see that there exists a group of people that are morally superior. I dream of not being in this group. Of the gratification of biting down on a juicy bloody burger and an airplane full of screams. But I also dream of being on this list and waking up on a planet far far away. I inform it and save many from cold refrigerators. I shiver.

3. After hearing of a lottery winner

It is recent. The air is bright and the palmtrees that I heard cost one hundred thousand dollars a year to water sway in the wind, which isn’t even that strong. I think of myself as a failure. If I had a billion dollars… I would buy 100 houses and rent them out for 3000 dollars a month each and make three hundred thousand dollars a month and that just sounds so nice. And of course I have to hire lawyers but I buy more and more properties and it just exponentiates.

4. High school and college

Those dreams of death come back sometimes but they are much less frequent. I also take any symptoms with a grain of salt the size of a mountain. Scared of cancer at having a headache but it’s always just a headache. Or when I coughed up blood it was just stress.

5. Anime

I am free. Arms stretch around wood that somehow doesn’t cut or burn and I fly onto the mast of a ship that faces only warm winds.

6. Another anime

I see her noticing the world around her but just starting to. And noticing that love that she failed to notice and when he dies she cries. I think of someone I love and my heart feels full and scared and I imagine performing whatever magic occurs in this world with them at my side because I can’t imagine anyone else and I can’t fathom being alone.

7. In college

I am young again. My life is draining and I have fears that I will no longer be able to end up where I end up. I study and make it places that I never could have imagined in a past life. maybe us imo team or winning isef or some novel research in high school I don’t know. but it’s draining without her. I am now me but three months ago. I still can’t imagine it. It makes me gut wrench in two and fear of losing what is important to me is more powerful than any emblem of prestige could be.

Problem 20 (calc 2)

\(\text{A curve }C\text{ is defined by the parametric equations }x=t^2, y=\frac{1}{3}t^3-t\)

a. \(\text{Find/simplify }\frac{dy}{dx}\text{ and }\frac{d^2y}{dx^2}\)

b. \(\text{Find the points on C where the tangent line is horizontal or vertical}\)

c. \(\text{Find the intervals in which the curve is concave up/down}\)

d. \(\text{Find any x-intercept(s) of }C\)

e. \(\text{Use the information from the above parts to sketch} C\)

f. \(\text{Set-up the integral that would find the length of the curve from }t=-1\text{ to }t=1\)
